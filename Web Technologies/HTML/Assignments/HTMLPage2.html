Tribute Page: Ratan Tata

Ratan Tata was an Indian industrialist and philanthropist, best known for his leadership of the Tata Group from 1991 to 2012. He transformed the group into a global business through strategic acquisitions and innovation.

Achievements

  • Chairman of Tata Group (1991–2012, 2016–2017).
  • Led acquisitions of Tetley, Jaguar Land Rover, and Corus Group.
  • Launched Tata Nano, the world’s cheapest car.
  • Received Padma Bhushan (2000) and Padma Vibhushan (2008).
  • Invested in over 40 startups and supported Indian entrepreneurship.
  • Known for his philanthropic work through Tata Trusts.
